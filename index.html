About Zenith HR Solutions

Your Strategic Talent Partner in India

Zenith HR Solutions is a leading Leadership Advisory firm that partners closely with corporates across India for their most critical leadership hiring needs. Founded in 2010 in Mumbai, we have grown into one of India's most trusted Executive Search firms.

Our client-centric approach, strict adherence to committed turnaround times, and high-quality processes have helped us build long-standing partnerships with large conglomerates, leading industry players, growing mid-sized corporates, and dynamic start-ups alike.

Our strict adherence to Six Sigma methodology and customised, bespoke solutions has resulted in diversified, long-standing relationships that stand the test of time.
